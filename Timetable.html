Mathematics Mastery

Studying all essential branches of mathematics to build a strong foundation:

  • Algebra: Linear equations, polynomials, matrices, and complex numbers.
  • Calculus: Limits, derivatives, integrals, and applications in physics.
  • Geometry: Euclidean, non-Euclidean, and coordinate geometry.
  • Trigonometry: Sine, cosine, tangent, and their real-world applications.
  • Statistics & Probability: Data distributions, inferential statistics, and stochastic processes.
  • Number Theory: Prime numbers, modular arithmetic, and cryptographic applications.
  • Discrete Mathematics: Graph theory, logic, and combinatorics.

Quantum Computing & Quantum Physics

Diving deep into the principles of quantum mechanics and computation:

  • Quantum Mechanics: Schrödinger's equation, wave-particle duality, and uncertainty principle.
  • Quantum Entanglement: Non-locality, Bell's theorem, and teleportation.
  • Quantum Computing: Qubits, superposition, and entanglement for faster computation.
  • Quantum Algorithms: Shor's algorithm for factorization, Grover’s algorithm for search.
  • Quantum Cryptography: Unbreakable encryption using quantum key distribution.
  • Applications: AI, optimization problems, drug discovery, and materials science.
